# DATAFLOW GUIDE: FROM CSV TO DASHBOARD

## 1. DATA INGESTION (THE "WRITE" PATH)
The application handles three primary types of CSV uploads from Schwab:

A. POSITIONS CSV (Schwab: "All Accounts - Positions")
   - Flow: Upload -> csv_parser.py -> enrichment.py (yfinance) -> pipeline.py -> Google Sheets
   - Destinations: 
     - 'Holdings_Current': Overwritten with latest state.
     - 'Holdings_History': Appended with a dated snapshot (deduplicated).
     - 'Daily_Snapshots': Appended with total portfolio value summary.

B. REALIZED G/L CSV (Schwab: "Realized Gain/Loss - Details")
   - Flow: Upload -> gl_parser.py -> pipeline.py -> Google Sheets
   - Destination: 'Realized_GL' tab (Appended with fingerprinting to prevent duplicates).

C. TRANSACTION CSV (Schwab: "History - Transactions")
   - Flow: Upload -> pipeline.py -> Google Sheets
   - Destination: 'Transactions' tab.

## 2. DATA ENRICHMENT (LIVE UPDATES)
On every import, the app calls external APIs to "flesh out" the data:
- yfinance: Fetches current Price, Sector, Asset Class, and Dividend Yield.
- Risk Engine: Uses 1-year historical price data to calculate Beta and Correlation.
- FMP API: Fetches earnings dates and transcripts for the Research Hub.

## 3. UI CONSUMPTION (THE "READ" PATH)
When you open the app, it reads from Google Sheets and populates the UI:
- Holdings_Current -> Main Dashboard (KPI Cards & Allocation Charts).
- Realized_GL -> Tax Page (Wash Sale warnings & YTD Gains).
- Daily_Snapshots -> Performance Page (Growth charts vs. S&P 500).
- RE Dashboard (External) -> Net Worth Page (NOI, Equity, and Debt).
