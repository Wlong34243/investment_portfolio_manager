# CORZ — Investment Thesis

## Style
Special Situation / High Beta / AI Compute Pivot

## Core Thesis
Buying a resurrected infrastructure play transitioning from a pure-play Bitcoin miner into a high-margin Artificial Intelligence / High-Performance Computing (HPC) landlord. Upside is driven by the massive, multi-billion-dollar deals (like the partnership with CoreWeave) to host AI GPUs, monetizing Core Scientific's most valuable asset: access to hundreds of megawatts of energized, contracted power. CORZ is a "picks and shovels" power play masquerading as a crypto stock. The key question is whether management can successfully execute the complex data center retrofits required for liquid-cooled AI servers without massive cost overruns. Judgment: yes, as a high-octane infrastructure satellite.

## Entry Context
Cost basis: $15.89. Buying at this level means capturing the stock after its successful restructuring and bankruptcy exit, heavily pricing in the initial wave of AI hosting contracts. At ~$16, the market is validating the transition away from pure Bitcoin mining volatility. The entry relies on the successful execution of these fixed-price AI hosting contracts to generate predictable, high-margin cash flows that command a higher multiple than cyclical crypto mining.

## Bull Case
- **The Power Moat:** In the AI arms race, compute (GPUs) is useless without power. Core Scientific controls massive amounts of contracted, energized power capacity across the US—an asset that takes legacy tech companies years and billions of dollars to replicate.
- **The CoreWeave Catalyst:** Securing multi-year, multi-billion-dollar hosting contracts with premier AI cloud providers (like CoreWeave) fundamentally transforms the income statement from volatile crypto earnings into predictable, utility-like recurring revenue.
- **Dual-Engine Model:** While pivoting to AI, CORZ still maintains a massive fleet of Bitcoin miners. If Bitcoin prices surge, the proprietary mining fleet acts as a high-beta cash flow engine to help fund the expensive AI data center retrofits.
- **Valuation Arbitrage:** Standard data center REITs (like Equinix) trade at massive premiums. If CORZ successfully proves it is a reliable AI infrastructure host, the stock should undergo a structural multiple expansion as it is re-rated from a "miner" to a "data center."

## Key Risks
- **Execution & Retrofit Risk:** Retrofitting a crypto mine (which essentially just requires fans and shelves) into a Tier-3 AI data center (requiring precision liquid cooling and immense networking) is incredibly complex and capital-intensive. Delays or failures here break the thesis.
- **Bitcoin Halving Economics:** The proprietary mining segment faces structurally lower rewards post-halving. If Bitcoin price drops, the mining operations could become cash-flow negative, draining capital needed for the AI pivot.
- **Customer Concentration:** Relying heavily on a few massive AI tenants (like CoreWeave) creates a single point of failure if those tenants face their own funding issues or default on contracts.

## Exit Conditions
- Management announces severe delays, cost overruns, or technical failures in retrofitting their facilities for AI workloads.
- A major AI tenant (like CoreWeave) breaks its contract or fails to pay, leaving CORZ with stranded infrastructure costs.
- The company issues massive amounts of dilutive equity to fund the data center buildouts, destroying shareholder value.
