# AMZN — Investment Thesis

## Core Thesis (The "100-Bagger" & Compounding View)
I am buying a multi-headed monopoly transitioning from a historic, capital-intensive investment cycle into a massive cash flow harvesting phase. Upside is driven by:
1. **Retail:** Structural margin expansion from regionalized fulfillment, turning retail into a scaled profit pool.
2. **AWS:** The foundational infrastructure layer for the AI boom, monetizing compute, storage, and platform services.
3. **Advertising:** A hyper-growth, high-margin engine compounding faster than overall GMV.

Earnings and free cash flow per share are expected to compound at a high-teens clip over my 3-year horizon. Risk is balanced by rising profitability and strategic dominance.
